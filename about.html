Our Story

Precision Piano has served midcoast Maine for years, tuning, repairing and restoring pianos for churches, music centers, halls and private clients from the coast inland to Bangor.

Precision Piano was founded by Frank Fischer, a graduate of the North Bennet Street School's Advanced Piano Technology program. Today the team includes technicians trained in that same tradition of hands-on craftsmanship, dedicated to keeping every piano we touch sounding its best.
