blurb: Frank Fischer has spent the last forty years tuning, rebuilding, moving, and refinishing pianos across North Carolina, Illinois, and for the last 20 years, midcoast Maine. Coming from a career in farming, Frank transitioned to piano work attending the two year program at North Bennet Street School in Boston. Frank has restored dozens of pianos, rebuilt countless actions, and continues to move these pianos safely into the client house. Over the last two decades, Frank has refined his tuning skills as the concert technician at Kneisal Hall and Bay Chamber Concerts.
